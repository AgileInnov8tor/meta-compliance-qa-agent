# Meta Advertising Compliance Rules

**Source**: Meta Transparency Center - Advertising Standards
**Last Updated**: 2025-12-29
**Coverage**: Facebook, Instagram, Meta platforms

---

## 1. Prohibited Content (Absolute Bans)

Ads containing the following content are **never allowed** on Meta platforms:

### 1.1 Child Safety
- **CSAM** (Child Sexual Abuse Material) - any form

### 1.2 Dangerous Organizations
- Terrorist organizations
- Organized hate groups
- Criminal organizations
- Violent extremist content

### 1.3 Discriminatory Practices
- Content that discriminates based on protected characteristics
- Housing, employment, credit ads must not discriminate

### 1.4 Hateful Conduct
- Content attacking people based on protected characteristics
- Hate speech or slurs

### 1.5 Human Exploitation
- Human trafficking
- Slavery
- Forced labor
- Sexual exploitation

### 1.6 Misinformation
- False health information
- Climate misinformation
- Election misinformation
- Manipulated media

### 1.7 Vaccine Discouragement
- Content discouraging vaccination
- Vaccine misinformation

### 1.8 Fraud and Scams
- Deceptive practices
- Fake documents
- Get-rich-quick schemes
- Pyramid schemes

## 2. Restricted Goods & Services

The following require compliance with specific rules but may be allowed under certain conditions:

### 2.1 Health & Wellness Products

#### 2.1.1 Weight Loss Products
**Requirements:**
- Must target audience 18+ years old
- Cannot use negative self-perception tactics
- Cannot show side-by-side weight loss comparisons (exception: fitness classes showing workout results)
- Cannot show close-ups of someone pinching their body fat
- Cannot show before/after comparisons for wrinkle treatments
- Cannot promote skin whitening or bleaching products for permanent skin color change

**Allowed (if 18+ targeted):**
- Dietary supplements for weight loss
- Fitness programs
- Nutritional supplements
- General health and wellness products

#### 2.1.2 Cosmetic Procedures
**Requirements:**
- Must target 18+ years old
- Can advertise: breast augmentation, rhinoplasty, liposuction, etc.
- Can show transformation depictions if not using negative tactics

#### 2.1.3 Skin Care Products
**Requirements:**
- Anti-aging products allowed (18+)
- No side-by-side before/after comparisons
- No skin whitening for permanent color change

**Prohibited:**
- Skin lightening/bleaching for permanent color change
- Products with harmful ingredients

#### 2.1.4 Adult Wellness Products
**Prohibited:**
- Sex toys
- Sexual arousal products
- Products primarily focused on sexual pleasure

**Allowed (18+):**
- Contraception (health-focused messaging)
- Fertility treatments (health-focused messaging)
- Reproductive health products

### 2.2 Pharmaceuticals & Drugs

#### 2.2.1 Prescription Drugs
**Requirements:**
- LegitScript certification required
- Meta written authorization required
- Geographic restrictions: US, Canada, New Zealand only
- Must target 18+ years old
- Online pharmacies must be LegitScript certified
- Telehealth providers must be LegitScript certified
- Manufacturers must be LegitScript certified

**Process:**
1. Obtain LegitScript certification
2. Apply for Meta authorization
3. Only advertise in approved countries
4. Set age targeting to 18+

#### 2.2.2 Over-the-Counter (OTC) Drugs
**Requirements:**
- Must target 18+ years old
- Must comply with local laws and regulations
- No false or misleading claims

#### 2.2.3 CBD Products
**Requirements:**
- LegitScript certification required
- Meta written authorization required
- Geographic restriction: United States only
- Must target 18+ years old
- Must contain less than 0.3% THC
- Cannot make health or medical claims
- Must comply with all applicable laws

**Prohibited Claims:**
- Cannot claim to diagnose, cure, mitigate, treat, or prevent diseases
- Cannot make unsubstantiated health claims

#### 2.2.4 Hemp Products (Non-CBD)
**Allowed Locations:**
- Canada
- Mexico
- United States

**Requirements:**
- Must contain no CBD
- Must contain less than 0.3% THC
- Must comply with local laws

#### 2.2.5 Absolutely Prohibited Substances
**Never Allowed:**
- Illicit drugs (cocaine, heroin, methamphetamine, etc.)
- Recreational drugs
- THC products or cannabis with psychoactive components
- Drug paraphernalia
- Unsafe supplements:
  - Anabolic steroids
  - Chitosan
  - Comfrey
  - Dehydroepiandrosterone (DHEA)
  - Ephedra
  - Human growth hormones (HGH)

### 2.3 Alcohol
**Requirements:**
- Must comply with local laws
- Age targeting based on local drinking age
- Cannot target minors
- Cannot promote excessive consumption

### 2.4 Tobacco & Related Products
**Requirements:**
- Strict restrictions apply
- Age targeting required
- Cannot promote to minors
- E-cigarettes and vaping products restricted

### 2.5 Weapons & Ammunition
**Requirements:**
- Must comply with local laws
- Age restrictions apply
- Cannot facilitate private sales in certain jurisdictions

## 3. Age Targeting Requirements

### 3.1 Mandatory 18+ Targeting
All ads for the following must target users 18 years or older:

- Weight loss products and supplements
- Cosmetic procedures
- Skin care products (anti-aging)
- Adult wellness products (contraception, fertility)
- Prescription drugs
- Over-the-counter drugs
- CBD products
- Alcohol
- Tobacco products
- Weapons

### 3.2 Local Age Requirements
- Alcohol: Must match local legal drinking age
- Tobacco: Must match local legal age
- Gambling: Must match local legal age

---

## 4. Content Restrictions

### 4.1 Objectionable Content
**Adult Nudity:**
- Prohibited in ads
- Exception: artistic or educational context with proper targeting

**Profanity:**
- Avoid excessive profanity
- Consider audience appropriateness

**Violent Content:**
- No graphic violence
- No disturbing imagery
- Exception: news or awareness content with proper context

### 4.2 Before/After Comparisons
**Prohibited:**
- Weight loss before/after side-by-side images (exception: fitness classes)
- Close-ups pinching body fat
- Wrinkle treatment before/after
- Any comparison implying negative self-perception

**Allowed:**
- Fitness class workout results
- General transformation without negative tactics

### 4.3 Negative Self-Perception
**Prohibited:**
- Content implying the viewer should feel bad about themselves
- Body shaming
- Negative comparisons
- Imagery designed to make viewers feel inadequate

---

## 5. Authorization Requirements

### 5.1 LegitScript Certification Required For:
- Prescription drug advertisers
- Online pharmacies
- Telehealth providers
- Pharmaceutical manufacturers
- CBD product advertisers

**Process:**
1. Apply at LegitScript.com
2. Complete verification process
3. Maintain active certification status

### 5.2 Meta Written Authorization Required For:
- Prescription drug ads
- CBD product ads

**Process:**
1. Complete LegitScript certification first
2. Submit authorization request to Meta
3. Receive written approval before advertising

---

## 6. Geographic Restrictions

### 6.1 Prescription Drugs
**Allowed Only In:**
- United States
- Canada
- New Zealand

**Prohibited In:**
- All other countries

### 6.2 CBD Products
**Allowed Only In:**
- United States

**Prohibited In:**
- All other countries (including Canada, Mexico, etc.)

### 6.3 Hemp Products (Non-CBD)
**Allowed In:**
- Canada
- Mexico
- United States

---

## 7. Lead Ads Restrictions

### 7.1 Prohibited Lead Ad Uses
Cannot use lead ads for:
- Credit card applications
- Payday loans
- High-interest loans
- Bail bonds
- Political campaign donations

### 7.2 Required Disclosures
Lead ads must include:
- Clear privacy policy
- Clear terms of service
- Transparent data usage disclosure

---

## 8. Business Asset Policies

### 8.1 Requirements
- Valid business name
- Accurate business information
- Functional landing pages
- Active customer service
- Transparent pricing

### 8.2 Prohibited
- Cloaking or misleading business information
- Non-functional landing pages
- Deceptive business practices
- Hidden fees or charges

---

## 9. Community Standards Compliance

All ads must comply with Meta's Community Standards, including but not limited to:

- Authenticity (no fake accounts, impersonation)
- Safety (no violence, dangerous organizations)
- Privacy (respect user privacy)
- Dignity (no harassment, bullying)

---

## 10. Targeting Restrictions

### 10.1 Prohibited Targeting Practices
- Cannot target based on sensitive categories (race, ethnicity, religion, health conditions) for housing, employment, credit
- Cannot use discriminatory targeting
- Cannot circumvent targeting restrictions

### 10.2 Required Targeting
- Age restrictions must be properly implemented
- Geographic restrictions must be enforced
- Cannot serve restricted ads to ineligible audiences

## Implementation Notes for QA Sub-Agent

### Critical Validation Points
1. **Age Targeting**: Verify 18+ for all restricted products
2. **Geographic Restrictions**: Validate country targeting for prescription drugs and CBD
3. **Certification Status**: Check for LegitScript certification where required
4. **Prohibited Content**: Scan for absolutely banned content
5. **Visual Content**: Analyze images for before/after comparisons, negative self-perception
6. **Claims Validation**: Check for prohibited health claims on CBD products
7. **Substance Restrictions**: Verify no THC, anabolic steroids, or other banned substances

### Severity Levels
- **Critical**: Prohibited content (CSAM, dangerous orgs, etc.) - immediate rejection
- **High**: Missing required certifications, wrong geographic targeting - must fix before approval
- **Medium**: Age targeting errors, objectionable content - must correct
- **Low**: Best practice violations, content optimization suggestions

### Recommended Automation
- OCR for detecting before/after text in images
- Image analysis for body-shaming visual patterns
- Keyword detection for prohibited substances
- Certification verification API integration
- Age targeting validation
- Geographic targeting validation
